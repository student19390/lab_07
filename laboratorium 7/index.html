Digital Art - what is it?
Digital Art / Digital Painting is an emerging art form in which traditional painting techniques are applied using digital tools by means of a computer, a graphics tablet and stylus, and software.

All digital painting programs try to mimic the use of physical media through various brushes and paint effects. This ability is very important in bridging the gap between traditional and digital painting.
